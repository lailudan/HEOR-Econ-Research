# N-of-1_Ludan: Vitals of a 40yo Primate

> **"Refusing to 'fuzz' through life. A Bayesian, math-driven audit of a Young-Onset Parkinson's journey, transforming personal history into structural evidence."**

---

## ## 1. Project Manifesto: The End of "Fuzziness"

For the first 40 years of my life as a "large-brained primate," I allowed many things to simply "fuzz by." However, when the biological algorithm of my dopamine system encountered a critical bug—**Young-Onset Parkinson’s Disease (YOPD)**—I decided to reclaim the narrative.

This repository is an **N-of-1 Trial**: a rigorous, longitudinal study of a single subject (myself). As a PhD researcher in Economics and Mathematics, I am no longer just a patient; I am the Lead Investigator of my own survival. I will dissect every neural perturbation, starting from the "cardiac detour" of 2024, with the precision of a system audit.

---

## ## 2. Core Research Scope

I do not merely record symptoms; I document **system variables**. This project aims to bridge the gap between subjective "blood and tears" and objective clinical data.

### 2.1 The Timeline (Data Reconstruction)
A high-fidelity reconstruction of the prodromal phase (2023) leading to the diagnostic odyssey of 2024. I am retracing my steps to identify the earliest "noise" in the system.

### 2.2 Doctor Visit Audit (Clinical Logic Analysis)
A systematic review of every specialist encountered. 
- **Variable:** Information Entropy. Did the consultation reduce uncertainty or add to the noise?
- **Variable:** Clinical Intuition. Who saw through the "primate mask" to identify the subtle neurological markers?

### 2.3 Narrative History (The Human Software)
The "blood and tears" translated into logic. Documentation of identity erosion, family dynamics, and the psychological "buffer overflow" that occurs when the body fails the mind.

### 2.4 Cognitive Compensation (Math vs. PD)
A unique record of how high-level cognitive activities (PhD research, complex mathematical derivations) act as a compensatory mechanism for dopamine deficiency. Does solving a stochastic differential equation provide a "neuro-protective" flow state?

---

## ## 3. Methodology

- **Bayesian Updates:** Every new medical report and medication adjustment serves as Evidence ($E$) to update the Posterior Probability ($P(H|E)$) of my long-term prognosis.
- **Digital Phenotyping:** Integrating wearable data (gait, heart rate variability) with subjective logs to map the physical boundaries of the "40yo Primate."
- **N-of-1 Experimental Design:** Treating medication changes as controlled interventions to observe the system's response latency and efficacy.
